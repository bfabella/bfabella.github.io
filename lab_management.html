Laboratory management

Over 25 years of institutional knowledge

Lab maintenance

Developed the institutional knowledge to solve any day-to-day problems that arose. Calibrated and maintained sensitive, scientific equipment. Organized annual spring cleanups. Worked with the purchasing department during inventory checks. Ordered supplies as needed to ensure research continuity. Collaborated with the lab's safety officer to ensure compliance during safety inspections.

Data management

Established and maintained the necessary infrastructure—upgraded ethernet connections, network-attached storage, backups, and archival—for a research group acquiring over hundreds of terabytes of data per year on multiple high-throughput instruments. Worked with the host institution to meet open access and data retention standards.

Capital equipment procurement

Responsible for procuring all equipment that cost more than $15000. Worked with the principal investigator to justify the equipment's cost and explain its purpose. Determined the most appropriate vendor and product to fulfill the researcher's needs. Solicited quotes, ordered the equipment, and oversaw delivery and setup.

Animal protocol development and compliance and husbandry

In order to comply with institutional, local, and federal regulations, developed the research protocols covering six different model species. Maintained a gecko vivarium. Responsibilities included housing, feeding, and health monitoring of up to six tokay geckos.

Controlled substances

Managed the acquisition, inventory, distribution, and disposal of controlled substances while following all regulatory requirements.

Personnel onboarding and offboarding

For all new lab members, facilitated the necessary orientations, including laboratory safety and animal care. Trained new employees on the proper and effective use of all lab equipment and the associated software. Upon departure, worked with each employee to curate their data and resources in order to guarantee research continuity and agreement with all open access and data retention policies.
